Ways to Lower Carbon Emissions When Traveling



Carpooling


Carpooling is a great way to work with coworkers to remove the need for driving many cars and causing higher amount of CO2 to be released.

Carpooling can also allow for better bonds with CoWorkers while benefieting the enviorment.


Taking Public Transit


Taking Public Transit on clean air vehicles, buses, or trains greatly reduces your carbon footprint by transporting many people on vehicle like a super carpool

Taking Public Transit can also reduce the cost of travel with a small fee to go each direction.


Biking


Biking to work takes CO2 pollution out of the equation which makes it a great alternative to driving.

It also provides a great workout that can help your personal health while also helping the enviorment.
